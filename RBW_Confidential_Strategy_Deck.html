05 · Market

AI Consumer Products Are Moving from Tools to Experiences

The first wave was productivity. The next wave is feeling, guidance and participation.
Wave 1 — AI as Tool
  • Writing assistants
  • Office and meeting copilots
  • Customer service automation
  • Code generation
Sold on efficiency. Replaceable by the next model.
Wave 2 — AI as Experience
  • Companionship and self-discovery
  • Social entertainment and group play
  • Wellness culture and education
  • Skill learning with guidance
Sold on emotion, guidance and participation. Sticky by design.
RBW thesis: Users no longer only want efficiency. They want emotional value, a sense of being guided, and the feeling of taking part in something. RBW is built for the experience layer of AI consumer products.

07 · Discipline

Each Product Has a Different Commercial Mission

Do not average effort across products. Each product must serve a clear commercial function.
不要平均用力，每个产品必须承担明确商业任务。
Vibe Party
Group Distribution
Brings users in groups, not one by one. Sponsored cards push traffic into other RBW products. KPI: rooms created, players per room, cross-product CTR.
RBW Meta
High-Margin Paid Reports
Structured self-discovery reports and follow-up questions. KPI: paid report conversion, AOV, 7-day return rate.
Acu Map
Wellness Education + SEO Trust
Compounding educational content for global users. KPI: SEO pages, organic traffic, time on page, email signups.
Magic Cube
Subscription Skill Learning
Beginner-to-solver guided path. KPI: practice days, formula completion, annual subscription conversion.
Rule: Every quarter, each product must demonstrate progress on its assigned commercial role — not just usage growth in general.

09 · Pattern

The Common Pattern: AI-Guided Experiences

Different categories. Same underlying experience design.
Self-discovery
Tarot · Xiao Liu Ren · Zi Wei Dou Shu — AI guides the user through a personal reflection arc that ends in a structured report.
Skill Learning
Magic Cube — AI guides a beginner through a 14-day path from "I don't know" to "I can solve a 3x3".
Wellness Education
Acu Map — AI guides the user through traditional Chinese wellness culture, acupoints and meridians as learning content.
Social Play
Vibe Party — AI and the system guide a group of people through a shared interactive session.
Conclusion: RBW does not sell tools. RBW sells the feeling of being guided through an experience.
RBW 不卖工具，RBW 卖的是被 AI 引导完成一段体验的感觉。

10 · Opportunity

Global Users Already Love Eastern Wisdom, Tarot, Wellness and Social Games

RBW is not creating new demand. RBW is entering categories with existing global willingness to pay.
Self-discovery
  • Western users already pay for tarot, astrology and oracle reading
  • Subscription apps in this space already exist at meaningful scale
  • Eastern systems (Xiao Liu Ren, Zi Wei) add differentiation
Wellness Education
  • Interest in acupuncture, acupressure, meridians and TCM wellness is rising globally
  • English-language educational content quality is uneven — clear positioning gap
  • Education framing, not medical claims, fits the global market
Social Play
  • Party games are mainstream in house parties, college events, bars and team building
  • QR-code group play is a proven viral mechanic
  • Hosts are natural distributors
Skill Learning
  • Cube solving is a global niche with stable demand
  • Parents pay for STEM and logic training
  • Annual subscriptions are well-accepted in this segment
RBW edge: Eastern cultural depth + AI-guided experience design + premium, non-cheap visual identity. This combination is rare in the current global market.
