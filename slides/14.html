Why We Should Not Burn Strategic Resources Too Early

Resource discipline is part of company risk control.
资源克制本身就是风控能力。
If We Burn Resources Too Early
  • Funnel is not proven — big traffic exposes broken steps publicly
  • Conversion path is immature — high-quality traffic cannot be retained
  • Resources do not accumulate as an asset, they evaporate
  • Partners lose confidence after one weak public test
  • Family governance signals weakened discipline
If We Validate First, Then Amplify
  • Small scenes reveal real user behavior cheaply
  • Funnel improvements ship before scale
  • Each resource activation is a measurable lift, not a gamble
  • Partners see numbers, not promises
  • Family governance signals mature operating discipline
Operating commitment: No strategic resource is activated until the relevant product reaches its pre-defined validation threshold. The threshold is decided before the test, not after.
