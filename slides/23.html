Commercial Judgement
The product is not just a formula library. It is a guided path. The value users pay for is the structured progression, the tracking and the small wins along the way — not the formulas alone.
Why subscription works here: Skill learning has a natural multi-month arc. Parents pay willingly for structured progress for their children. Adult learners pay for hobby progression. Annual plans are well-accepted in this category.
