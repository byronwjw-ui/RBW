How to Explain RBW to Investors

One sentence. Same sentence. Every time.
Do not say
“We made tarot, cube, acupoints and party games.”
This sounds like four small apps and limits the conversation immediately.
Say this
“RBW is building an AI Experience Engine that turns cultural knowledge, social entertainment, wellness education and skill learning into guided interactive products.”
RBW 正在打造一套 AI 互动体验引擎，把文化内容、社交娱乐、健康科普和技能学习变成可商业化、可传播、可复用的互动产品。
Supporting Lines
  • Vibe Party creates group distribution.
  • RBW Meta monetizes self-discovery.
  • Acu Map builds wellness education trust.
  • Magic Cube creates subscription learning.
  • One shared engine ships all of them.
