90-Day Validation Plan

Three windows. Each one has named deliverables and pass / fail conditions.
Days 1 – 15 · Build Surface
  • Overseas presence pages live
  • This HTML deck delivered
  • Vibe Party usable build
  • RBW Meta report sample
  • Acu Map English content sample
  • Magic Cube challenge sample
Days 16 – 45 · Live Test
  • Vibe Party: 30 – 50 sessions
  • RBW Meta: first paid cohort
  • Acu Map: 30 SEO pages
  • Magic Cube: ¥99 / $9.99 subscription test
  • Structured user feedback
Days 46 – 90 · Decide
  • Vibe Party: 100 real sessions
  • Cross-promotion data formed
  • Meta paid funnel optimized
  • Domestic ICP launch prepared
  • Small-scale B2B sales attempted
  • Decision: activate strategic resources or extend validation
Gate principle: If validation passes, strategic resources are activated. If validation fails on a specific product, that product’s budget is paused, not its sibling products. Discipline at the product level, not the company level.
